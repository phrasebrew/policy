# PhraseBrew Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "Policy") sets forth how Silvis, LLC (hereinafter referred to as "Company") handles users' personal information and related data in the iOS app "PhraseBrew" (hereinafter referred to as "Service").
Please read this Policy carefully before using the Service.

---

## Article 1 (Information We Collect)

The Company collects the following information in providing the Service:

1. **Information obtained through Google login**

   - Username
   - Profile picture
   - Google account ID (for billing management via RevenueCat)

2. **Information stored on the device during app use**

   - Japanese text entered by users
   - Translation results and key phrase explanations
   - Usage history (number of translations, etc.)

3. **Billing information**

   - Paid plan subscription status (including Apple ID billing information)
   - Payment-related information managed by Apple Inc. or RevenueCat (the Company does not directly obtain credit card information)

---

## Article 2 (Purpose of Information Use)

The Company uses the collected information for the following purposes:

1. Providing, operating, and improving the Service
2. Managing in-app purchases (using RevenueCat)
3. User support and inquiry response
4. Preventing unauthorized use and violations of terms
5. Sending important notifications as necessary

---

## Article 3 (Handling of Translation Data)

1. Translation results and input text are stored only on the user's device (AsyncStorage and iOS Files app backup).
2. The Company does not collect, store on servers, or use translation data for learning purposes.
3. Translation processing uses Google's "gemini-2.5-flash."
4. Translation content is not used for Google's learning.
5. However, translation content may be stored or cached by Google or its agents for a certain period to detect content that violates Google's terms.

---

## Article 4 (Third-Party Services)

The Service uses the following third-party services. Each service handles data according to its own privacy policy:

1. **Google Login (Firebase Authentication)**
   - Used for user authentication
   - Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. **RevenueCat**
   - Used for managing subscription billing
   - Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

3. **Google Gemini API**
   - Used for translation processing
   - Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## Article 5 (Data Retention Period)

1. Data stored on the device is retained until the user manually deletes it or uninstalls the app.
2. Data managed by RevenueCat is retained according to RevenueCat's privacy policy.
3. Google account information is retained while the user continues to use the Service.

---

## Article 6 (Data Security)

1. The Company implements appropriate security measures to protect users' personal information.
2. Translation data is encrypted and stored on the device.
3. Communication during Google login uses secure protocols (HTTPS).

---

## Article 7 (Provision of Information to Third Parties)

The Company does not provide users' personal information to third parties without user consent, except in the following cases:

1. When required by law
2. When necessary to protect a person's life, body, or property and obtaining user consent is difficult
3. When particularly necessary to improve public health or promote the sound growth of children and obtaining user consent is difficult
4. When cooperating with government agencies, local governments, or their contractors in executing affairs prescribed by law and obtaining user consent may hinder the execution of such affairs

---

## Article 8 (User Rights)

Users have the following rights regarding their personal information:

1. Request disclosure of personal information
2. Request correction of incorrect personal information
3. Request deletion of personal information
4. Request suspension of use of personal information

For the above requests, please contact us using the contact information in Article 11.

---

## Article 9 (Use by Minors)

This Service cannot be used by persons under 18 years old. When minors aged 18 to under 20 use the Service, parental consent is required.

2. Changed Policy becomes effective from the time it is posted within the Service.
3. Users who continue to use the Service after changes to the Policy are deemed to have agreed to the changed Policy.
