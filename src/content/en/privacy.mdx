# Phrase Brew Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "Policy") sets forth how Silvis, LLC (hereinafter referred to as "Company") handles users' personal information and related data in the iOS app "PhraseBrew" (hereinafter referred to as "Service").
Please read this Policy carefully before using the Service.

---

## Article 1 (Information We Collect)

The Company collects the following information in providing the Service:

1. **Information obtained through Google login**

   - Username
   - Profile picture
   - Google account ID (for billing management via RevenueCat)

2. **Information stored on the device during app use**

   - Japanese text entered by users
   - Translation results and key phrase explanations
   - Usage history (number of translations, etc.)

3. **Billing information**

   - Paid plan subscription status (including Apple ID billing information)
   - Payment-related information managed by Apple Inc. or RevenueCat (the Company does not directly obtain credit card information)

---

## Article 2 (Purpose of Information Use)

The Company uses the collected information for the following purposes:

1. Providing, operating, and improving the Service
2. Managing in-app purchases (using RevenueCat)
3. User support and inquiry response
4. Preventing unauthorized use and violations of terms
5. Sending important notifications as necessary

---

## Article 3 (Handling of Translation Data)

1. Translation results and input text are stored only on the user's device (AsyncStorage and iOS Files app backup).
2. The Company does not collect, store on servers, or use translation data for learning purposes.
3. Translation processing uses Google's "gemini-2.5-flash."
4. Translation content is not used for Google's learning.
5. However, translation content may be stored or cached by Google or its agents for a certain period to detect content that violates Google's terms.

---

## Article 4 (Provision to Third Parties)

The Company does not provide users' personal information to third parties without user consent, except in the following cases:

1. When user consent is obtained
2. When required by law
3. When necessary to protect a person's life, body, or property and obtaining user consent is difficult
4. When providing to contractors within the scope necessary for service operations (e.g., billing management via RevenueCat)

## Article 6 (Data Retention Period)

1. **Device Data**: Translation results and input history are stored on the device (AsyncStorage) until manually deleted by the user.
2. **Backup Data**: Translation history backups saved in the iOS Files app are retained until deleted by the user.
3. **Usage Limit Management Data**: Weekly usage counts for the free plan are retained on the server for 7 days and then automatically deleted.
4. **Subscription Information Cache**: Billing status obtained from RevenueCat is cached on the server for up to 5 minutes.
5. **Upon Account Deletion**: When logging out of a Google account, translation data on the device is not deleted and can be restored upon re-login. If complete deletion is desired, please delete manually from within the app.
